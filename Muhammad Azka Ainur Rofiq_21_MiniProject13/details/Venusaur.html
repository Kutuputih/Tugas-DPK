Venusaur #0003

"The Seed Pokemon"

Venusaur is a squat, quadrupedal amphibian Pok√©mon with bumpy, blue-green skin. It has small, circular red eyes and a short, blunt snout. Its mouth is wide with two pointed teeth in the upper jaw and four in the lower jaw. On top of its head are small, pointed ears with reddish-pink insides. It has three clawed toes on each foot. A female Venusaur has a gynoecium in the center of its flower.
