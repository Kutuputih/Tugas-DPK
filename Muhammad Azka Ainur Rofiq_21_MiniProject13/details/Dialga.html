Dialga #0483

"The Temporal Pokemon"

Dialga has the power to control time; it can warp time by either speeding it up, slowing it down, reversing it, or stopping it altogether. It can also travel through time to visit the past, present, and future. Additionally, it is capable of blasting its targets with power that distorts time itself. It also has the power to destroy and create an entire universe, either by itself or with Palkia's aid.


Pokedex Entries

It has the power to control time. It appears in Sinnoh-region myths as an ancient deity.
